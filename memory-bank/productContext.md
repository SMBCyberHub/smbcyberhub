# Product Context - SMBCyberHub

## Unique Selling Proposition (USP)

SMBCyberHub provides privacy-first, downloadable cybersecurity compliance kits for small businesses. Our core differentiators:

- **One-time download** - No subscriptions or recurring fees
- **No logins required** - Privacy-focused, no account creation
- **Audit-ready in 30 minutes** - Quick implementation framework
- **Offline-first** - Kits work without internet access
- **No SaaS dependency** - Complete control over your data
